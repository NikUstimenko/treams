ptsa.TMatrixC.rotated¶

TMatrixC.rotated(phi, **kwargs)¶

Rotate the T-Matrix around the z-axis

Rotation is done in-place. If you need the original T-Matrix make a deepcopy first. Rotations can only be applied to global T-Matrices.

Parameters:

  • phi (float) – Rotation angle

  • modes (array) – While rotating also take only specific output modes into

  • account –

Returns:

T-MatrixC
